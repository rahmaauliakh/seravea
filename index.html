About Us

Seravéa, founded on October 8, 2025, was created with a simple but meaningful purpose: to support people who struggle with sensitive skin by offering products that are gentle, safe, and naturally effective. Our formulas are crafted from carefully selected natural ingredients and developed under dermatologist supervision to ensure quality you can trust. The name “Seravéa,” inspired by the words “Serenity” and “véa,” reflects our commitment to calmness, protection, and everyday comfort. Through our products, we aim to bring confidence and peace of mind to everyone on their skincare journey.

Vision & Mision

Seravéa’s vision is to become a trusted skincare brand that offers safe, gentle, and effective solutions for sensitive skin through natural ingredients and dermatologist-approved innovation.
